Frequently Asked Questions 
  

Questions
  • Q1: How do I book times in a classroom for faculty meetings?
  • Q2: How do I look up an instructor?

Answers
  • Q1: How do I book times in a classroom for faculty meetings?
      • Set it up as a 900 level course called Faculty Meetings with time and room that should be booked for these meetings.

  • Q2: How do I look up an instructor? One of my instructors has been teaching for us already, but I have not been able to look her PUID up by name.
      • If you cannot find an instructor (who has already been teaching here) by last name, try his/her career account.
